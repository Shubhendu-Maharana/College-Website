Affiliation

Affiliation and Academic Partnerships

Rajiv Memorial Group of Institutions is proud to be affiliated with esteemed academic institutions, ensuring a robust foundation for quality education and industry relevance. Our programs are aligned with recognized authorities, providing students with qualifications that hold significance in the academic and professional spheres.

BCA and BES Programs

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) and Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Communication (BES) programs are affiliated with the prestigious Berhampur University. This affiliation reflects our commitment to delivering a curriculum that meets the stringent standards set by a renowned academic institution, emphasizing academic excellence and industry relevance.

Diploma and ITI Programs

The Diploma and ITI programs at Rajiv Memorial Institute of Technology hold affiliation with the State Council for Technical Education & Vocational Training. This affiliation underscores our dedication to providing practical, hands-on learning experiences in fields such as civil, computer science, mechanical, electrical, and more, aligning our curriculum with industry demands.
