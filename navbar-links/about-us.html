About Us

Welcome to RMIT Group Of Institutions, located in the vibrant town of Konisi, Berhampur! Established with a commitment to fostering excellence in education, RMIT stands as a beacon of knowledge and innovation. We take pride in offering a diverse range of courses to cater to the varied interests and career aspirations of our students. Whether you're passionate about Computer Applications (BCA), Environmental Studies (BES), seeking specialized skills through our Diploma programs (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical & Computer Science), or pursuing hands-on technical training with our ITI courses, RMIT Group Of Institutions provides a dynamic learning environment that encourages intellectual growth and real-world application. Our dedicated faculty members, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive community contribute to a holistic educational experience. At RMIT Group Of Institutions, we believe in shaping well-rounded individuals equipped with the skills and knowledge to excel in their chosen fields. Join us on a transformative journey as we strive to empower the next generation of leaders and innovators.
